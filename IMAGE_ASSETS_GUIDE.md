Recommended format:

- Use landscape photos for hero usage, ideally around `1600x900`
- Use `PNG` if you want to keep the naming consistent with the current app setup
- Keep file names lowercase with hyphens
- Use a transparent `PNG` for the logo

Current placement in the app:

- `logo.png` is shown on the splash screen, language selection screen, and home header
- destination images are now used in destination cards and destination detail heroes
- activity images are now used in activity cards and activity detail heroes
- accommodation images are now used in stay cards and stay detail heroes
- dining images are now used in the Eat & Drink cards
